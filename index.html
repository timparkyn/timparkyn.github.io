Some things about my riding

I'm a late morning and afternoon rider

Some people like to wake up early and ride. I like to have some coffee, breakfast, think about the day a bit, and then go ride.

Fair weather rider

I like to ride when it's nice and warm. When it's cold, I'll do something else, go for a run or something.

I don't race

I don't care if I'm faster or slower than anyone else. It's not a competition. But please don't hold me up on the downhills.
